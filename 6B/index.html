What are mocktails?

A mocktail is a non-alcoholic drink that is created to be similar to a cocktail. Everything from the ingredients (except the alcohol) to the appearance makes a mocktail look almost identical to the cocktail inspired by it. In this sense, mocktails can be thought of as alcohol-free alcohol drinks.

Rhubarb Rose Lemon Fizz

Example recipe (Source) for the Rhubarb Rose Lemon Fizz:
  • .75 oz fresh squeezed, strained lemon juice
  • .75 oz rhubarb syrup
  • 3 drops rose water
  • Tonic water, to top
  • Optional: egg white

Instructions

Instructions for the Rhubarb Rose Lemon Fizz: Shake the lemon juice, rhubarb syrup, rose water and egg white in a cocktail shaker filled with ice. Strain into a small, chilled glass (a coupe or a bistro glass works well) and top with tonic water (about 2 ounces).
